About Us

आप के विश्वास पर खरा उतरना ही हमारा काम है |

At 24Carat, we celebrate the rich cultural heritage of Rajasthan through timeless jewelry pieces. Our artisans handcraft every design—from the classic Boriya Nath to elegant jhumkas—with techniques passed down through generations. We’re not just selling jewelry—we’re sharing stories, traditions, and the vibrant soul of India’s royal history.

Whether you’re dressing up for a wedding, festival, or a special occasion, our pieces ensure you stand out with regal elegance.
